Dog Care

Here are some general instructions on how to care for your dog:

  • Provide a balanced diet appropriate for your dog's age, size, and activity level.
  • Ensure regular exercise and mental stimulation.
  • Regular visits to the veterinarian for vaccinations, check-ups, and preventive care.
  • Maintain good dental hygiene by brushing your dog's teeth regularly.
  • Keep your dog's living environment clean and safe.

For more detailed information, you can visit American Kennel Club - Dog Care.

Cat Care

Here are some general instructions on how to care for your cat:

  • Provide a balanced diet appropriate for your cat's age, size, and activity level.
  • Ensure regular exercise and playtime to keep your cat active and healthy.
  • Regular visits to the veterinarian for vaccinations, check-ups, and preventive care.
  • Keep your cat's litter box clean and provide a comfortable living environment.
  • Offer scratching posts and toys to satisfy your cat's natural behaviors.

For more detailed information, you can visit ASPCA - Cat Care.
